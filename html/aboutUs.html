What we are about...

We are a small group of passionate junior developers who care about the environment and want to use technology to do our part. Our app "How Recycle" was develped in a 5 week period with the main goal of educating anyone on how to recycle properly. We all believe recycling is something that can make a big impact but only a few of us take the time to learn how to, we want to help change that by making all the information more accessible.
